Chart Types
===========

Candlestick
-----------

.. image:: images/chart-types/candlestick.png
   :align: center

Displays the Open, High, Low, and Close price.


Candlestick Trend
-----------------

.. image:: images/chart-types/candlestick-trend.png
   :align: center

Displays the Open, High, Low, and Close price. The previous candle is used when determining color.

:doc:`Coloring algo <candlestick-trend-chart-type>`


Hollow Candlestick
------------------

.. image:: images/chart-types/hollow-candlestick.png
   :align: center

Displays the Open, High, Low, and Close price. Bullish bars are hollow, bearish bars are filled.


Real Body
---------

.. image:: images/chart-types/real-body.png
   :align: center

Displays the Open and Close price.


OHLC
----

.. image:: images/chart-types/ohlc.png
   :align: center

Displays the Open, High, Low, and Close price.


HLC
---

.. image:: images/chart-types/hlc.png
   :align: center

Displays the High, Low, and Close price.


HL
--

.. image:: images/chart-types/hl.png
   :align: center

Displays the High and Low price.


Area
----

.. image:: images/chart-types/area.png
   :align: center

Displays the Close price. The area from the baseline (zero) to the price is filled.


Line
----

.. image:: images/chart-types/line.png
   :align: center

Displays the Close price.


Colored Line
------------

.. image:: images/chart-types/colored-line.png
   :align: center

Displays the Close price. The line segments between each point are colored based on increase or decrease in price.


Step
----

.. image:: images/chart-types/step.png
   :align: center

Displays the Close price.


Histogram
---------

.. image:: images/chart-types/histogram.png
   :align: center

Displays the Close price.


Dots
----

.. image:: images/chart-types/dots.png
   :align: center

Displays the Close price.


Invisible
---------

.. image:: images/chart-types/invisible.png
   :align: center

Displays nothing. Use this if you have a study applied and are not interested in the price.


Heikin-Ashi
-----------

.. image:: images/chart-types/heikin-ashi.png
   :align: center

Displays an averaging of current and previous bars.


Column
------

.. image:: images/chart-types/column.png
   :align: center

Displays values in columns, similar to Histogram but Column have width.

*Only used by studies*
