Version 5.1.1
=============

Beantrader Latinum version 5.1.1 is a bugfix release.

The most important changes are listed in this document.

### Drawing
   * Improved performance (reduced lag) when moving drawings or
     editing anchor points

### Studies
   * Fixed studies that draw Trend lines. The following studies
     were affected; Pivot Points, Closed Trades.

### News Feed
   * Changed server address for retrieving default news feeds
