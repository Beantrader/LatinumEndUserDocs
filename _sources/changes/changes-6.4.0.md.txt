Version 6.4.0
==============

Beantrader Latinum version 6.4 contains bug fixes and new features.

The most important changes are listed in this document.

### Charts
   * Fixed potential crash loading charts with an applied data range

### Datafeed
   * Added data feeds providing chart data for 17 new exchanges: ACX,
     Bibox, Bit-Z, Bitbank, BitBay, bitFlyer, Bithumb, Bittrex, CEX.IO,
     Kraken, Kucoin, LBank, Luno, Qryptos, QuadrigaCX, Quoine, and
     ZB.com. These data feeds do not use streaming prices, they update
     every 5 or 10 seconds (depending on the feed)
