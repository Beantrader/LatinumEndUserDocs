Version 5.2.7
=============

Beantrader Latinum version 5.2.7 is a bugfix release.

The most important changes are listed in this document.

### Trading
   * OANDA
      * Added sound effects for trade, close trade, order complete,
        and order fail events
      * Added high and low values to mini order ticket

### Datafeed
   * FXCM
      * Fixed issues parsing market data (e.g. empty instrument name)
