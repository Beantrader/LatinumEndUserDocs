Version 6.6.2
=============

Beantrader Latinum version 6.6.2 is a bugfix release.

The most important changes are listed in this document.

### Charts
   * Fixed keyboard shortcuts sometimes not working

### Studies
   * Fixed last price marker not updating for Inverse Pair study

### Datafeed
   * BINANCE
      * Fixed some reconnection issues
   * HUOBI
      * Added support for HUOBI10 Index and HB10 ETF symbols
   * OKCOIN
      * Added OKCoin (not CN) charts, trade, and order data. This offers
        fiat-to-crypto data compared to crypto-to-crypto from OKEx.
