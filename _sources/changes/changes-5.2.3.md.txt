Version 5.2.3
=============

Beantrader Latinum version 5.2.3 is a bugfix release.

The most important changes are listed in this document.

### Drawing
   * Fixed Horizontal Ray tool label, keep it within the viewable area

### Chart Trading
   * OANDA
      * Fixed 'Invalid Precision' error when chart trading
      * Fixed updating of stop loss and take profit level markers
        when trade or order price has changed
