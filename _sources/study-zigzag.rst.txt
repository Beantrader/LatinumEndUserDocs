Study Attributes
----------------

.. image:: images/study-zigzag-props.png
   :align: center

Reversal %
  The minimum percentage change in price since the previous zig for a zag to be added.

  *A value of 5.5 equals 5.5%.*

Pricing Method
  The price field used to calculate the change amounts.

Test Order
  The order of test operations.
